How WL Refinement Works

1. Initial Coloring: Vertices are colored according to their degrees.

2. Aggregation: At each step, a vertex gathers its neighbors' colors as a sorted multiset, appends its own color, and hashes them to generate a new color.

3. Check: If the multisets of vertex colors of both graphs differ, they are guaranteed non-isomorphic.
